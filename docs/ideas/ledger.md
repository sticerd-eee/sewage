# Idea Ledger

Single source of truth for the research ideation funnel
(see `docs/plans/2026-07-04-001-research-ideation-funnel.md`).
Every idea gets a row the moment it is proposed. Scores are 1–5
(5 exceptional, 4 good, 3 moderate, 2 weak, 1 poor); advancing requires
at least 4 on all three criteria.
