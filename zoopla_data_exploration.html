6.1 Data Description

I spatially merge Zoopla rental listings (2021–2023) to nearby sewage discharge sites within multiple distance thresholds (e.g., 250m, 500m, 1km, 2km, 5km). For each rental, I summarise spill severity (counts and duration) over either all years or the prior 12 months and compute distances to the matched sites. This enables cross-sectional analyses by distance threshold and spill severity.

6.2 Rental Descriptive Statistics
